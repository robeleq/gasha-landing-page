Some of the best features Of Our App!

Case Stats

Provides the number of cases, recoveries, and deaths from novel coronavirus in Ethiopia.

Map View

Provides Regional case counts with map view.

Self Check

COVID-19 self-check tool with CDC recommendations.

Tips

Symptoms, Protections, Free Numbers, and relevant tips from WHO and the Ministry of Health Ethiopia.

Get Notified

Provides Up-to-date information from the Ministry of Health's notifications to stay informed.

Easy To Use

Beautiful interface with simple navigation and customizations.

Location Tracking

Monitor your route. The location history is saved on the users device only.
